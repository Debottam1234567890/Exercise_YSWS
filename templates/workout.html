FAQ

Do I need equipment?

A webcam and gravity. That's the whole list.

Is this actually free?

100% free. No credit card. No ads. Just sweat.

How do you track my exercises?

We run a 3D pose-estimation model directly in your browser. It maps 33 body landmarks in real-time — shoulders, elbows, wrists, hips, knees, ankles — and sends those coordinates to our AI classifier.

Can I cheat?

You can try. Our AI flags unusual patterns (like impossibly fast reps or zero body motion), and human reviewers check every high-tier cash-out before prizes ship.

What exercises are supported?

Squats, push-ups, bicep curls, shoulder presses, planks, yoga poses (tree, lotus, mountain), jumping jacks, and more. We add new ones every month.

How long until I can cash out?

That depends on how hard you grind. A sticker pack is 500 Watts — about a week of casual workouts. The world cup tickets? That's a marathon, not a sprint.

Do you store my webcam footage?

No. Your camera feed never leaves your browser. We only receive skeleton landmark coordinates — 33 pairs of numbers per frame. No images, no video, ever.

Can I use my phone instead of a laptop?

Right now Cyber Gym is optimised for laptop / desktop webcams. Mobile support is on our roadmap for Q4.

What happens if my internet drops mid-workout?

Your reps are buffered locally. When the connection comes back, the app syncs everything automatically. You won't lose a single Watt.
